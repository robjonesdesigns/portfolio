# ADR-005: Burgundy/Rose Accent Color

**Date:** 2026-03-25
**Status:** accepted
**Deciders:** Rob Jones

## Context

The accent color needed to work with the warm cream palette, feel calming with a touch of luxury, and avoid the cultural heaviness of pink. Rob's personal style leans toward pink (visible in portfolio photo), so the accent needed to complement that without being pink directly.

## Decision

Burgundy (`#813746`) for light mode, rose (`#e36f86`) for dark mode. Both are in the pink family but read as mature and professional. The burgundy pairs with the cream background to create a warm, luxurious feel. The rose variant maintains the brand family while meeting accessibility contrast requirements on dark surfaces.

## Consequences

- 7.78:1 contrast ratio on light mode (AAA)
- 4.81:1 contrast ratio on dark mode (AA)
- Cohesive with Rob's photo and personal brand
- The color avoids the pastel pink associations (too casual) and hot pink associations (too aggressive)

## Alternatives Considered

- **Pastel pink:** Debunked calming research, cultural heaviness, risk of not reading as professional.
- **Navy/blue:** Safe but generic for a design portfolio. Every other portfolio uses blue.
- **Auburn/orange:** Calming and proven, but already taken by a coworker.

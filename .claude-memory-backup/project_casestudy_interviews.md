## Plan
Rob wants Claude to interview him on each case study project so the content in projects.js (WorkEntry headlines, descriptions, outcomes) and CaseStudy pages (overview, problem, process, solution, outcome) can be filled with real, accurate content.

After each interview Claude should recommend what visual assets would best tell the story for that case study.

## Projects to cover (in projects.js)
1. Honeywell APM — enterprise SaaS, dashboards, 3 years
2. Aysa MVP — 0→1, corporate transparency app, 2 weeks to launch
3. Keytrn Proptech — 0→1, proptech/foreclosure data platform
4. Sinta HR Platform — 0→1, next-gen HR interview platform

## How to run the interview
- One project at a time
- Ask about: the problem they were solving, their specific role, the process they followed, key decisions made, outcomes/results, what they're most proud of
- After each project: recommend asset types (screenshots, videos, wireframes, before/after, flows, etc.) that would best illustrate the story

Then update src/data/projects.js with the refined content.
